Shall create 3d surface from parametric functions

Definitions:

Parametric functions are written as 3 equations like:

  • x = v * Math.cos(u)
  • y = v * Math.sin(u)
  • z = v

Descriptions:

This requirement is implemented with the Equation object's generate points function.
